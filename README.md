# 🧠 PBY Lab — Perception-Based Yonder

> **Exploring AI for a better world beyond today.**

Welcome to **PBY Lab** – *Perception-Based Yonder*.  
We are a future-facing AI research lab dedicated to building intelligent systems that perceive the world deeply and act with purpose — to advance science, sustainability, and society.

---

## 🌍 About Us

PBY Lab focuses on cutting-edge research in:

- 🧠 **Computer Vision & Perception** – Making machines truly see and understand the world.
- 🔬 **Transformers & Representation Learning** – Learning from massive, complex, multi-modal data.
- 📊 **Ethical & Explainable AI** – Transparent, interpretable models for real-world trust.
- 🌱 **AI for Sustainability** – Leveraging intelligence to tackle global challenges.
- 🛰️ **Remote Sensing & Earth AI** – Extracting insights from satellite and environmental data.

Our philosophy is grounded in building **human-centered**, **ethically-aligned**, and **future-proof** AI systems.

---

## 🚀 Vision

We believe that perception is only the beginning.  
At **PBY Lab**, we look *yonder* — beyond accuracy, beyond benchmarks — to create AI that **benefits people and planet**.
