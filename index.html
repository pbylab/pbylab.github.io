Teerapong Panboonyuen

Ph.D. in Computer Engineering, Chulalongkorn University

Dr. Kao currently serves as an AI researcher at MARSAIL (Motor AI Recognition Solution Artificial Intelligence Laboratory). He is a recipient of the prestigious Postdoctoral Fellowship (Track A) from the Second Century Fund (C2F) at Chulalongkorn University. He is one of the select Thai researchers awarded the esteemed Global Young Scientists Summit (GYSS) 2025 scholarship, generously conferred by Her Royal Highness Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn. His research focuses on representation learning, optimization theory, and self-supervised learning.
